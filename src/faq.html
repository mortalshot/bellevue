Aks a question
How can I use the SPA area?
The wellness area is open from 10:00 to 22:00 daily, with extended hours on Mondays and Fridays from 17:00 to 22:00. Access is available only for hotel guests and must be requested at the Hotel reception; please make a reservation at least an hour in advance. The price for accessing the wellness area is €10.00 per person for 1.5 hours. Children under 7 years of age can use the wellness area free of charge, while children from 8 years old are charged €10.00. Please note that children can only stay in the wellness area when accompanied by an adult.
